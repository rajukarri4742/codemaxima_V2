Spy Number

A number is said to be a Spy number if the sum of all the digits is equal to the product of all digits. 
  For Example:  
               Consider a number, 22
               sum = 2 + 2 = 4
               product = 2 * 2 = 4
  Here, sum of all the digits in 22 and product of all the digits in a number 22 is equal. 
               So, 22 is a spy number.
               Consider a number, 34
  Here, sum of all the digits in 34 and product of all the digits in a number 34 is not equal. 
               So, 34 is not a spy number.
              
Input:
22
Output:
Spy number
